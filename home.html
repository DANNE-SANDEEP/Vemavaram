Welcome to, Vemavaram

Welcome to Vemavaram, a village in Ballikurava Mandal, Prakasam district, Andhra Pradesh. With a population of 1,901 across 476 families, the village has a balanced male-to-female ratio. The community practices both Hinduism and Christianity, reflecting its diverse cultural heritage.

Clinical Health Project

Focused on 9th-10th graders and middle-aged villagers, this initiative offers vitals and CPR training while educating about common diseases. The project donates vital equipment and provides long-term educational materials to ensure ongoing health awareness.

Learn More

Reproductive Health Project

This project focuses on 8th-9th grade girls, providing them with education on menstruation and establishing a period pantry. It creates an open dialogue around reproductive health and partners with NGOs to ensure sustainable restocking and management of the pantry.
